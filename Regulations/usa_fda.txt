US FDA Pharmaceutical Website Compliance Checks

CHECK 1: Safety information must be clearly visible.

CHECK 2: Marketing must present fair balance between risks and benefits.

CHECK 3: Claims must be supported by clinical data.

CHECK 4: Pages must include a link to full prescribing information.

CHECK 5: Marketing must not imply guaranteed cure.

CHECK 6: Drug claims must match approved labeling.

CHECK 7: Safety warnings must be clearly identifiable.
