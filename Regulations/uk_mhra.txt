UK MHRA Pharmaceutical Website Compliance Checks

CHECK 1: Safety information must appear above the fold.

CHECK 2: Benefit claims must be supported by clinical evidence.

CHECK 3: Risk information must appear alongside benefit claims.

CHECK 4: Pages must include a link to full prescribing information.

CHECK 5: Claims must match approved drug indications.

CHECK 6: Marketing must not exaggerate treatment outcomes.

CHECK 7: Important Safety Information must be clearly labelled.
