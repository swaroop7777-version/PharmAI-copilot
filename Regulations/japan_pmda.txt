Japan PMDA Pharmaceutical Website Compliance Checks

CHECK 1: Advertising must match approved drug indications.

CHECK 2: Marketing claims must not exaggerate effectiveness.

CHECK 3: Safety warnings must be clearly visible.

CHECK 4: Claims must reference approved medical data.

CHECK 5: Risk disclosure must appear alongside benefit statements.

CHECK 6: Advertising must be scientifically accurate.

CHECK 7: Information must not mislead patients or physicians.
